Beginner's Brushpen Calligraphy Workshop - ₹1300

🌈 5 Days • 1.15 hours/day + Q&A • Online - Google Meet

Materials Required:

  • Worksheets (PDF), Brushpens (Doms/Camlin/Flair)

What You'll Learn:

Advance Brushpen Calligraphy Workshop - ₹2800

12 Days • 1 Hour/day + Doubt Solving • Online - Google Meet

What You'll Learn:

  • Guidelines, strokes, bouncy/regular calligraphy
  • Upper/lowercase, connections, quotes
  • Shadow lettering, blending, watercolor backgrounds

Materials Required:

  • Pencil, eraser, notebook, brush pens (small/large)
  • Plain paper >120gsm, watercolor, flat brush

7 Days • One-to-One • 1 Hour/day + Q&A • Online - Google Meet
